Consider the following student, feepayment and department tables.
The administration team would like to get the studentid, name and deptname of students who have paid the semester fee along with the amount paid.
  • Observation
    • The required information is scattered across three tables.
    • A common field studentid is present in student and feepayment table.
    • A common field deptid is present in student and department table.
  • How can the admin retrieve the required data?
By Joining Tables
