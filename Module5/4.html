• Cartesian Product of the records / rows from two or more joined tables
  • Syntax is as follows
SELECT <<* or column list>> FROM <<table1>> CROSS JOIN <<table2>>;
  • Every row from table1 will be combined with all rows of table2
  • If table1 has m rows and table2 has n rows then the output will have m*n rows
