Human brain understands the things around us by the way of logical classiﬁcation or categorization

For example- birds, animals, ﬁshes, countries, cities, furniture, organizations, machines, etc.

Such logical abstraction results in creation of entities. For example : Bird, Fish, Country etc.

An Entity has properties and behaviors.

Every bird will also execute behaviors like ﬂy, eat, lay eggs, etc.
