InfoTech Systems is creating an online application for automating the task of job search between employer and job
seekers. This application would be used by employers to post their company’s job openings and job seekers to apply
for suitable jobs. In addition to this, InfoTech Systems also aims at organizing an online preliminary assessment to
check jobseekers eligibility based on test scores and making it available to recruiting organizations.
This will help
those organizations to search and identify the right talent that they are looking for.
